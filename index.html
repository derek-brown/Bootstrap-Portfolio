About Me

Hello! My name is Derek Ethan Brown. I am a up and coming web developer with experience in HMTL, CSS, Javascript, and JQuery. I am in the process of fine-tuning my craft through the University of Central Florida (UCF) Full Stack Coding Boot Camp. My goal is to be proficient at both front end and back end developments of the web.

In 2014, I graduated with a degree in Technical Communications and a minor in writing and rhetoric. Since college, I have worked as a Multi-Line insurance sales representative until present. It is my goal to transistion my career from insurance to be a full time Full Stack Developer. Much of my work now is self taught through mediums as Udacity and Code School.

In my freetime, I stay very active. I enjoy running, swimming, camping, hiking, and most of all rock climbing. I help organize the largest Climbing group in Orlando, the Orlando Rock Climbing and Bouldering Meetup Group. We regularly meet every Monday and Wednesday at 6:30pm at Aiguille Rock Climbing Gym in Longwood, FL with occasional self-arranged meetups by members.

I aim to remain consistently involved in the Orlando scene for Meetups and networking groups of the aforementioned. I enjoy being around, getting to know, and assisting people from all walks of life. In my pursuit of career, I will actively purpose bringing people together because when people talk great things happen.
